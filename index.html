Introduction.

Columbus is a data processing system designed to support the feature selection process. Analytics is one of the biggest and most important topics in data management, and feature selection is regarded as one of the most crucial step in it. Columbus is developed to aid the analysts in this feature selection task so as to help them in better and more efficient exploration of their data. It provides a library of several common feature selection operations which are present in the literature and used by analysts these days.
